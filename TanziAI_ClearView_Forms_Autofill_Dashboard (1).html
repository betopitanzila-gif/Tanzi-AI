What this extracts
The reader uses the actual DOCX tables, not OCR. For joint RFFs it looks at the Page 1 rows such as Name, Date of Birth, Address, Preferred mobile phone and Preferred Email address.
Rule: Life insured / person insured / policy owner name fields use the selected extracted client’s full name. DOB, address, suburb, state, postcode, contact number and email are mapped to matching ClearView form fields. Signature fields and signature date fields are always left blank.
